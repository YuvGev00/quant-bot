Thesis & key risk

Thesis

A genuine early-stage turnaround: down ~44% from its high to a level it last saw a decade ago, with the first hard evidence the CEO-Hill 'Win Now' plan is working — North America revenue +3% with wholesale +11%, and February sell-through grew across every channel for the first time in two years. Q3 EPS beat by ~20%, analysts hold a Buy (36) with ~+35% upside, and forward EPS is set to trough then recover. This is the MU/Dell-style 'left-for-dead leader regaining shelf space' pattern.

Key risk

The recovery is early and unproven: ROE has collapsed to ~4% and gross margin is being squeezed by U.S. tariffs, Greater China revenue is guided down ~20%, and RBC just cut Nike to Sector Perform ($50), pushing the payoff to 2027. June 30 earnings are a binary catalyst (~10% implied move).
