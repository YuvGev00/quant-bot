Thesis

The most beaten-down name (~-49% from high, bottom 2% of range) and statistically the highest-quality (ROE ~32%, net margin ~37%, strong FCF, rising EPS). Key risk

The decline is at least partly justified, which is why this is CAUTION not CONFIRM: (1) a real structural question — generative AI (Canva, OpenAI/Midjourney image gen) threatens the Creative Cloud moat; (2) management just chose to deliberately slow near-term revenue by ~$500M via a freemium pivot, and the stock fell ~25% on 'blowout' results; (3) a leadership vacuum — both the CEO and the CFO are departing within months; (4) analysts are split (29 Hold, 4 Sell) and Evercore just moved to In Line. Needs the freemium bet and AI monetization to prove out before conviction.
